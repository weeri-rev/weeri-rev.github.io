It's the people who MAKE the place

Hi, according to my passport, my full name is Master Weeraphon Iamnak (i-am-nak). My friends just call me Weeri.

I travel and make videos. Not just any kind of travel video. My focus is on the beauty of people in their habitat, because I believe that the world would be a more happy place when people have more empathy for one another.

By traveling, experiencing, listening, learning and sharing I want to discover how people work, sleep, eat and spend their free time.

I want to share my journey with you and hopefully inspire you to travel some more.
